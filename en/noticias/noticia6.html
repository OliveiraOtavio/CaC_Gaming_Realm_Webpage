Game Jam 2023

The GCBA, through the "Plan BA Gaming" program, generated the initiative to create gaming-themed videogames. inclusion. The initiative was organized by the Ministry of Economic Development and Production, through the General Directorate of Youth Policies of the City of Buenos Aires, aims to make visible the talent from local game developers and give them the opportunity to show off their prototypes. During the month of March, the participating teams, made up of young people from all over the country, were asked between 16 and 35 years old, the development of a video game, thought from a gender perspective, in just ten days.

Looking for gamification to help create spaces for social awareness, the axes with which we worked in this edition were: urban planning with a gender perspective, economic empowerment of women and expansion of women's rights.

There were many participants but only 3 games were chosen that were awarded. If you want to be part of this, you can choose which one seemed best to you or you liked the most.
