"The Magesekeer" is here

On April 18, Riot Games in conjunction with Digital Sun (creators of Moonlighter) released "The Mageseeker" is an arcade-style action RPG that takes place in the League of Legends universe.

In the kingdom of Demacia, Mage Hunters use their power to oppress magic users. in the name of public order. To do this, they kidnap and indoctrinate them, lock them up or force them into exile. Take on the role of Sylas, a spell-thieving wizard who has just escaped the clutches of the Spell Hunters. wizards escaping from their unjust captivity. Brandishing the chains that once oppressed you, you must save your home of tyranny, hunter after mage hunter.

The game will be available for: PC, Xbox (series X/S), PS4 and PS5, Epic Games, GOG.com and Nintendo Switch.

Game Features:

• Spell Steal
Magic is everywhere, and it's yours if you want it. From searing flames to deadly ice, learn and cast spells that fit your personal play style. Wield your chains and use the magic of your enemies against them, or beat them with your fists: the choice is yours.
• Start a rebellion
Your numbers will increase with each mission. Use the power of your allies to upgrade your base and unlock unique abilities.
• Master the magic
Channel the magic of your enemies by wielding the chains that were used to imprison you and use it against they. It's not stealing if you pay it back more than enough.
• Fists of fury
Teach Demacia what power means! Master a combination of melee attacks, both fast and light as heavy and brutal, and take down your enemies.
