Are you a gamer and don't know what career to study?

Playing video games a few times a week helps build better decision-making skills important or to solve any type of problem that arises in daily life. Therefore these are some points that will help you decide a career.

In a Surrey research, in collaboration with Game Academy Ltd, they have conducted a study to look at in detail the behavior of individuals who spend much of their time playing video games for Internet. This by keeping in mind that according to these people they usually have greater skills than those who do not They do these kinds of activities.

For several years it has been possible to demonstrate that video games are not as bad as many think or believe. have made believe throughout our lives. In fact, there are already several scientific studies that have It has been shown that people who practice these activities tend to have a better IQ or better answers than those who do not enjoy any game.

The truth is that this type of entertainment in people is capable of offering a large amount of benefits such as improving coordination when doing some activities, having greater abilities to decision-making or to solve any problem of daily life.

To carry out the test, the Steam platform and a video game store have been used so that could enjoy several of the titles available there. In total, among all the participants were used the 800 games that were used the most during the study. In addition, the researchers only added to the participants that their gender and occupation were known.
