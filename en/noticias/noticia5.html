Hoyoverse releases a new gacha

On 04/24 the “Honkai: Star Rail” was launched, the new gacha of the Chinese company. It is an action MMORPG anime style where your characters are valkyries, warriors of the Schicksal (the most powerful organization in the planet Earth) and your ultimate goal is the destruction of humanity. In the game, you will be the captain in command of These Valkyries and you will have to face humanity's greatest enemy by their side.

This game is only available in 4 languages ​​(English, German, Chinese and French) and can be played on PC and Smartphones downloading them from their respective platforms.

For more information, visit the official website.
