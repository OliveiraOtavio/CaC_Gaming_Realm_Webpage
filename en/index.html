GAME OF THE MONTH

FEATURED GAMES

Darkest Dungeon 2 is an action role-playing and adventure video game, sequel to the cult title from Red Hook Studios. In this second part, the combat system from the first game will return, although it has been improved in terms of mechanics and presentation.

Far Cry 6 is a new video game from Ubisoft's first-person action saga. In the title we will travel to Yara, a fictional tropical paradise ruled by a tyrant: Anton Castillo, whose face and voice are given by Giancarlo Esposito.

The sequel will take us back to an extensive Hyrule although much darker and more twisted after the return of an evil incarnated by Ganon

Street Fighter 6 is the new installment of Capcom's veteran 2D fighting saga, which returns remade with Capcom's RE ENGINE technology to give it greater spectacularity. Its game modes are divided into World Tour, Fighting Ground and Battle Hub.

Diablo 4 is the fourth installment of the Blizzard action and role-playing saga, an institution in the world of videogames. This time he will return to his roots, presenting us with a world of gothic horror and dark fantasy, returning to the constant fight against demons and the creatures that hide in the underworld.

The Lord of the Rings: Gollum, will be an action and adventure video game focused on the character of Gollum for Xbox One, Xbox Series S/X, PS4, PS5, Nintendo Switch and PC, narrating the events that transpired just after obtain the One Ring.
